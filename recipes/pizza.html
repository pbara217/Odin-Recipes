Mediterranean Harvest Delight Pizza

Description:

Experience the taste of the Mediterranean with our "Mediterranean Garden Harvest Pizza." A masterpiece of flavors, this pizza boasts a tomato-sunrise base adorned with roasted garlic, caramelized red onions, colorful bell peppers, tender zucchini, black olives, and artichoke hearts. The crumbled feta cheese crowns this culinary canvas, creating a harmonious blend that transports you to coastal villages. Each bite is an exploration of the region's essence, capturing the essence of land and sea in a single slice.

Ingredients

  • Pre-made pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Roasted garlic cloves
  • Roasted red pepper strips
  • Caramelized red onions

Directions

  1. Preparation: Preheat your oven according to the pizza dough package instructions. Roll out the pre-made pizza dough onto a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  2. Assembling the Pizza: Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly over the pizza dough. Scatter roasted garlic cloves and caramelized red onions across the sauce. Arrange slices of bell peppers, zucchini, black olives, and artichoke hearts over the sauce.
  3. FBaking and Serving: Bake the pizza in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted. Remove the pizza from the oven and sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the toppings. Slice the pizza and serve, savoring the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean in every bite.
